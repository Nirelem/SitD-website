Elinhir

Elinhir, historically known as the "City of Mages", is a city in eastern Hammerfell, close to the border with both Skyrim and Cyrodiil.

  • The city is noted for its massive Apex Towers of Nedic design.

  • The Apex Stone of the Mage, a powerful remnant of the Nedic religion, is located beneath the city.

  • The city is home to the great academy of the Blackcaster Mages Guild.

  • Elinhir is the oldest surviving city in Craglorn, and is known for its use of magical techniques to make everyday tasks easier.

  • The city is ruled and protected by the Blackcaster Mages, who were formerly a group of rogue mages.

  • The city is an ideal place for mages to conduct their studies and experiments, being close to the Nedic ruins and the Apex Stone of the Mage.

  • Despite its initial reputation as a haven for outcasts of the Empire, the city became relatively "civilized" under the rule of the mages.

  • Elinhir was caught up in the War of Bend'r-mahk, having been claimed by the Nords of Skyrim.

  • Although resistance in Elinhir was feeble at best, there was much more conflict on the border regions in the countryside.

  • Whether the city is still occupied today is unknown.
