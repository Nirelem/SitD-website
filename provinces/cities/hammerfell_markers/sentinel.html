Sentinel


Sentinel during 2nd Era in Elder Scrolls Online

Sentinel is a great city of the Redguards, and the capital of both the kingdom of Sentinel and the province of Hammerfell. It is located in the northwestern hills of Hammerfell, overlooking the Iliac Bay.

Architecture & Layout: Its population is relatively low due to the inhospitable nature of the surrounding Alik'r Desert. However, some fertile fields can be found along the coast near the city, and the city is actually a major population center in Hammerfell. Sentinel contains a structure called the Impervious Vault â€” a fortified storehouse under the city that houses the Ansei Wards, ancient Redguard relics that ward off necromancy.

Politics: The city is an exotic retreat for the nobility of Daggerfall and Wayrest, who delight in the cooking, craftsmanship, and plays which can be found there. It has historically been a Forebear city.

Quick History: In 4E 42, Altmer dissidents who had fled to Sentinel from the Thalmor (the high elf n*zis) in Alinor were attacked by Thalmor operatives in what became known as the Night of Green Fire. The entire refugee district of the city was destroyed. An Altmer legionary named Legate Fasendil was stationed there at the time and commented, "The Aldmeri Dominion was not content with killing dissidents at home, so they went to Hammerfell to finish the job."
