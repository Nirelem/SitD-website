Hew's Bane

Hew's Bane is a peninsula in southern Khefrem on the south coast of Hammerfell. The peninsula starts at the isthmus found south of Gilane and north of Prince's Gate.

The peninsula is home to exotic species such as the kotu gava and haj mota, as well as more mundane creatures like lions, lamia, and assassin beetles.

The largest settlement on the Peninsula is the city of Abah's Landing.

Abah's Landing

Abah's Landing, the Jewel of Khefrem, is a harbor city on the eastern coast of Hew's Bane in southern Hammerfell.

  • The city is settled in a strategic location with a naturally protected harbor.

  • The city is a major port on the Abecean Sea.

  • While the city is ruled by a monarch, royal power in Abah's Landing has been entirely supplanted by a group of criminal cartels known collectively as the "Merchant Lords".

  • The hinterlands of Hew's Bane were populated primarily by smuggler crews and pirates, with whom the Lords actively did business.

  • The city-state of Abah's Landing thus developed a deep rivalry with the kingdom of Taneth to the north, whose traders usually bore the brunt of this thievery they harbored.
