Taneth

Taneth is one of the eight major cities in the province of Hammerfell.

  • The city is situated on the coastal highlands of southern Hammerfell.

  • The ancient tomb of the al-Danobia bloodline is located northwest of the city and it is considered part of the Kingdom of Taneth.

  • Taneth is established on the eastern side of Hew's Bay and is equipped with a harbor.

  • Taneth is a bustling and important port city.

  • Taneth is strongly a Forebear city, but the Crown al-Danobia bloodline had always held significant power over the Kingdom for many untold years.

  • Beyond the Danobia bloodline, Taneth is the home of several noble families, such as House Suda and the Moguls, the latter of which is held in high prestige and is famous for its cavalry.

  • Taneth is located on a major western trading route utilized by the East Empire Company; through this route, it is connected to the cities Rihad and Hegathe.

  • Trade going to Taneth, either through the mainland or through the sea has always been affected by the merchant lords of Abah's Landing in Hew's Bane, who tend to hire pirates or bandits to interfere in the traffic in Taneth.

  • During the Great War, the Aldmeri Dominion conquered the southern coast of Hammerfell, excluding the city of Hegathe.

  • When the Empire signed the White-Gold Concordat they ended the war between them and the Dominion, but had to abandon Hammerfell in the process.

  • Five years later, the Redguards reclaimed the land from the Dominion, and ended the war between them by signing the Second Treaty of Stros M'Kai.
