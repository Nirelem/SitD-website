Orsinium

Orsinium is the City of the Orcs. It has been sacked and rebuilt many times over the centuries. The current iteration of Orsinium (also called The Fourth Orsinium) is located somewhere in the mountains between Hammerfell and Skyrim.

  • The previous three iterations of Orsinium were all located within High Rock.

  • Each time Orsinium was sacked, it was done so by an alliance between High Rock and Hammerfell (The Bretons and Redguards may be rivals, but they can unite when it comes to destroying the City of Orcs).

  • The latest incarnation of Orsinium, which was settled in the Fourth Era, is the first one to be located within Hammerfell — it is guarded by the Seventh and Fifteenth Imperial Legions.
