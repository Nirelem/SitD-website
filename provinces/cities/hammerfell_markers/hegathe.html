Hegathe

Hegathe is one of the eight major cities in the province of Hammerfell.

  • The city is found in the fertile grasslands on the tropical western shore landings of the Abecean Sea.

  • The entire peninsula that the city itself is found upon is a lush subtropical rainforest biome.

  • The city is known for its austere ramparts that harken back to antiquity, and is considered an equal to Wayrest in terms of splendor.

  • Like other traditional cities in Hammerfell, it is built atop of old ruins of past civilizations, replaced with high domes and flying dew sails that are well-suited for the harsh environment.

  • It is a city of contrasts with tall, plain stone ramparts found side-by-side with beautiful gold-engraved tents, and a busy modern harbor found near the calm ancient shrines.

  • Being on the southern part of Hammerfell, it is probably under the influence of the Forebears.

  • Hegathe was once the capital of Hammerfell, ever since the Yokudans landed on its shores back in the First Era.

  • Thanks to its coastal plains environment, the city has vineyards where they make their signature wine.

  • One of the city's most iconic landmarks is the Shrine of Tava (Redguard deity, Goddess of the weather), a place of veneration for both the Crowns and Forebears.

  • This religious site symbolizes an ideal: the union of the two factions.

  • During the Great War, Hegathe was being defended by a Crown army.

  • When it was besieged by Aldmeri Dominion forces, a relief army of Forebears arrived and lifted the siege.

  • This victory brought the reconciliation of the two parties and was start of the tenuous peace that they have today.
