Skaven

Skaven (also known as Ska'vyn) is one of the eight major cities in the province of Hammerfell, situated in the region of Sunforge in central Hammerfell.

  • The city is located near the Dragontail Mountains, in a region that is prone to frequent sandstorms and scorching heat.

  • The city reveres Tu'whacca (Redguard God of Souls) and contains a great necropolis.

  • On the northern outskirts of Skaven lies the Caverns of Wry, which contains herds of Skav Bats that are bred to produce dairy by the locals.

  • The city contains a headquarters for visiting caravans.

  • Skaven is a Crown city, and their old traditions are especially prevalent in this city.

  • The city is situated in hostile desert lands but is kept alive by its use as a trading hub connecting the cities of Dragonstar and Elinhir to the east.

  • Despite the desolate surroundings the city is located in, the city has a culture of festivities, known for its carnivals and Firedancers.

  • During the Great War, the Imperial 14th Legion, under General Decianus had a bloody skirmish with the Aldmeri Dominion, led by Lady Arannelya, outside of Skaven's wall, but the city was captured by Dominion forces.

  • General Decianus was being ordered to return to Cyrodiil and assist in its defense, but, fortunately, the casualties from the battle for Skaven left Lady Arannelya's forces unable to move out and continue their campaign for the time being.

  • Before the general returned to the Imperial City in 4E 174, he discharged many soldiers that later formed the core of fighters that retook Skaven from the Dominion and drove Arannelya's force back across the Alik'r Desert.
