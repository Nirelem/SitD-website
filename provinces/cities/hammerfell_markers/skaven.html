Skaven


Skaven during 3rd Era in Elder Scrolls I: Arena

Skaven (also known as Ska'vyn) is one of the eight major cities in the province of Hammerfell, situated in the region of Sunforge in central Hammerfell.

Architecture & Layout: The city is located near the Dragontail Mountains. The region the city is located in is prone to frequent sandstorms and scorching heat. The city reveres Tu'whacca (Redguard God of Souls) and contains a great necropolis. On the northern outskirts of Skaven lies the Caverns of Wry, which contains herds of Skav Bats that are bred to produce dairy by the locals.

Politics: The city is situated in hostile desert lands but is kept alive by its use as a trading hub connecting the cities of Dragonstar and Elinhir to the east. The city even contains a headquarters for visiting caravans. Despite the desolate surroundings the city is located in, the city has a culture of festivities, known for its carnivals and Firedancers. Skaven is a Crown city, and their old traditions are especially prevalent in this city.

Quick History: During the Great War, the Imperial Legion, under General Decianus had a bloody skirmish with the Aldmeri Dominion outside of Skaven's wall, but the city was captured by the Aldmeri Dominion. However, Lady Arannelya's forces were unable to move out and continue their campaign. The general was then ordered return to Cyrodiil and assist in its defense. Before the general returned to the Imperial City in 4E 174, he discharged many soldiers that later formed the core of fighters that retook Skaven from the Dominion and drove Arannelya's force back across the Alik'r Desert.
