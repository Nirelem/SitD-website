Gilane

Gilane is one of the eight major cities in the province of Hammerfell, and is located on the south-central coast near the Abecean Sea, across the isthmus toward Hew's Bane.

  • Gilane is situated just south of the Alik'r Desert; further south is an isthmus that connects the mainland with the Hew's Bane peninsula.

  • Gilane is specifically on the west side of the isthmus, on the large bay it shares with Hegathe where it has a port.

  • Being on the southern part of Hammerfell, it is probably under the influence of the Forebears.

  • During the Great War, the Aldmeri Dominion conquered the southern coast of Hammerfell, excluding the city of Hegathe.

  • When the Empire signed the White-Gold Concordat, they ended the war between them and the Dominion, but had to abandon Hammerfell in the process.

  • Five years later, the Redguards reclaimed the land from the Dominion, and ended the war between them by signing the Second Treaty of Stros M'Kai
