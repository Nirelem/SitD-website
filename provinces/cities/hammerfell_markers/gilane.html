Gilane


Gilane during 3rd Era in Elder Scrolls I: Arena

Gilane is one of the eight major cities in the province of Hammerfell, and is located on the south-central coast near the Abecean Sea, across the isthmus toward Hew's Bane.

Architecture & Layout: Gilane is situated just south of the Alik'r Desert. Just further south is an isthmus that connects the mainland with the Hew's Bane peninsula. Gilane is specifically on the west side of the isthmus, on the large bay it shares with Hegathe where it has a port.

Politics: Being on the southern part of Hammerfell, it is probably under the influence of the Forebears.

Quick History: During the Great War, the Aldmeri Dominion conquered the southern coast of Hammerfell, excluding the city of Hegathe. When the Empire signed the White-Gold Concordat, they ended the war between them and the Dominion, but had to abandon Hammerfell in the process. Five years later, the Redguards reclaimed the land from the Dominion, and ended the war between them by signing the Second Treaty of Stros M'Kai.
