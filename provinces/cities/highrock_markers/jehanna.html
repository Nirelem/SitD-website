Jehanna

Jehanna is one of the historical Breton kingdoms in the province of High Rock, situated on the icy shores of the Northern Coast.

  • Jehanna lies on the northeast coast of High Rock, right near the border of Skyrim, in a generally northern biome.

  • Nestled in the snow-covered forests of the Western Reach, it is described as being "as beautiful as its name".

  • The Jehanna Road is a major roadway that runs near the Bjoulsae River in Bangkorai and rolls northeast into the Wrothgarian Mountains.

  • For most of its early history, the land that makes up the core of Jehanna was mostly underdeveloped and ignored; it started out merely as a small, isolated dock.

  • It was only in 2E 864 that the name Jehanna started appearing as a mark on maps of High Rock.

  • In the late 3rd Era, during the War of the Bend'r-mahk, the lands of Jehanna had fallen under the control of Skyrim

  • Currently, the state of the kingdom and the city are unclear at best, but most assume that the lands are considered to be a disputed territory between High Rock and Skyrim.
