Isle of Balfiera


Isle of Balfiera, 2nd Era in Elder Scrolls Online

The Isle of Balfiera is an island region within the northern portion of Iliac Bay. The isle is most renowned as the site of the Adamantine Tower, the oldest known structure in Tamriel and currently the last holdout of the Direnni Elves, who were once the masters of High Rock.

According to lore, the Adamantine Tower was the site for where the Gods convened and discussed the creation of the world. This would make the tower both the oldest structure in the world, and the place where the world began.

Balfiera was known as a neutral meeting place for the kingdoms of High Rock, who used it to facilitate negotiations and treaties.
