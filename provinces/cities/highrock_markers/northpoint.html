Northpoint

Northpoint (also spelled as North Point) is a Breton kingdom in the province of High Rock, located on the northeast point of Rivenspire.

  • Northpoint is located at a very strategic point in Rivenspire, hosting the only port in the entire region, where the Eltheric Ocean meets the Great Bay.

  • Northpoint sits at the top of Dore Elard, a mountain that overlooks the coast and provides a grand vista of the ocean and the beaches that surround it.

  • The landscape around the city consists of grassy plains with privately-owned farmsteads and large rocky outcrops between the coast and the river in southern Boralis.

  • At the walled city's center is a large fountain and the seat of power, Dorell Manor.

  • Northpoint's three main roads disperse toward the western gate, the southern gate, and Lanthorn, which is the cty's signature lighthouse.

  • South of Dorell Manor is the city's graveyard and along the southern walls are large towers.

  • Some of the city's biggest buildings are located in the center, including the inn and the smithy, and further out are manor houses and average homes.

  • The marketplace is located along the northeastern wall of the city.

  • Northpoint is an important trading center along the northwest trading route on the Eltheric Ocean, as well as the seat of power for House Dorell, one of the three major houses of Rivenspire.

  • Their power extends across northern Rivenspire, including farmland around the city and beyond the sea.

  • When the Warp in the West took place in 3E 417, the kingdom of Northpoint took advantage of the chaos to claim territory around them, and emerged as one of the five major Breton kingdoms after the Warp.

  • Their expansion was careful to avoid confrontation with the kingdoms of the Iliac Bay (Daggerfall and Wayrest) or even Skyrim's forces in the post-War of the Bend'r-mahk.
