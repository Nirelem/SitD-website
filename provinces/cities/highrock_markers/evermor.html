Evermor

Evermor, sometimes spelled Evermore, is a city on the banks of the Bjoulsae River in eastern High Rock, located near the border with Hammerfell.

  • Its impressive palace was assembled from slabs of granite quarried from nearby Markarth.

  • The city is strategically located along the only land route that connects High Rock to the rest of the continent of Tamriel.

  • Historically, lands around Evermor were subject to frequent attacks from the Reachmen of the mountains, who asserted their birthright to the city and aimed to reclaim their ancestral homeland.

  • Because Southern Bangkorai is considered a region of Hammerfell, Evermore's rulership is often contested by the Redguards of Sentinel.

  • Evermore's position along trade routes spanning to the rest of Tamriel allowed it to become an important trading hub.

  • Locally, it linked eastern High Rock, the southern and central regions of Hammerfell, Orsinium, and western Skyrim, while more distant trade routes to the southeast connected it with Cyrodiil.

  • After the Warp in the West, which took place during the conclusion of Elder Scrolls II: Daggerfall, The Kingdom of Evermor was one of the five major Breton kingdoms that emerged after the Warp.
