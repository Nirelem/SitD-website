Farrun

Farrun (or Fharun) is a northern port city on the north shore of High Rock. It is known for its icy winters.

  • The Breton kingdom of Farrun has existed since the First Era.

  • By the Second Era it was an Orcish stronghold by the name of Fharun, named after the Fharun Clan.

  • Fharun returned to being the Breton city-state of Farrun, where an unnamed king was known to have fought a war against Solitude and its allies some time late in the Third Era.
