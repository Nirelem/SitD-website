Throat of the World

The Throat of the World is the highest mountain in all of Tamriel. Seven thousand steps lead up the mountain to its summit and the settlement of High Hrothgar, monastery of the Greybeards.

The Greybeards are an ancient and monastic order of honored monks who live in silence in their monastery, High Hrothgar. They study the Way of the Voice and are masters in the use of the Thu'um, a powerful form of magic that originated from the ancient Nords and uses the words of the Dragon Language to form "Shouts", the equivalent of spells of immense power.
