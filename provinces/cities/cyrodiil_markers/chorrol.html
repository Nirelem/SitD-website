Chorrol

Chorrol is a Colovian town in northwestern Cyrodiil, between the Great Forest of west Cyrodiil and the Colovian Highlands of the Hammerfell border.

  • The city is protected by thick walls and guard towers.

  • The city is divided into five main districts: Fountain Gate, Castle Chorrol, Chapel Street, West District, and the Great Oak Place.

  • Chorrol also possesses its own sewer system.

  • The city is the seat of County Chorrol.
