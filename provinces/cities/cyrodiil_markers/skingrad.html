Skingrad

Skingrad is a Colovian town located in the West Weald region of Colovia, to the southwest of the Imperial City.

  • The city divides into three districts: the Castle, Hightown, and Chapel, with various gates and bridges connecting each district.

  • The city is further divided in two parts: the north wall encompasses the Hightown district and the guild halls, while the southern half holds homes and the Great Chapel of Julianos (God of Magic), where citizens and travelers can pray for healing.

  • Castle Skingrad sits on a very steep hill, overlooking the city, and is only accessible by a long series of fortifications and bridges that connect it to the Hightown district.

  • Due to the sheer steepness of the hill around Castle Skingrad making it virtually unscalable, this makes Castle Skingrad and the city as a whole very defensible against outside forces.

  • The city is the seat of County Skingrad.

  • The city is particularly famous for its wines, tomatoes, and cheeses, which contributes to its overall prosperity.

  • The famous Surilie Brothers wine is made from the vineyards located outside Skingrad.

  • During the Great War in 4E 171, Skingrad was one of the cities to fall to the Aldmeri Dominion's, invasion.

  • When the Great War ended, the Dominion relinquished all conquered territory.
