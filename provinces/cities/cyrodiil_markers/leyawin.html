Leyawin

Leyawiin is a Nibenese city in the extreme southeast of the province, by the mouth of the Niben River.

  • Known as the city that spans the waters, the city straddles a swampy estuary of the Niben on the small strip of land between Elsweyr and Black Marsh.

  • Leyawiin is roughly divided into four districts: Chapel District, Guild Plaza, Main Street and Castle Leyawiin.

  • The city is the seat of County Leyawin.

  • During the Great War in 4E 171, Dominion forces marched out of hidden camps in Northern Elsweyr.

  • Leyawiin was one of the first cities to fall to the Aldmeri Dominion's invasion.

  • When the Great War ended, the Dominion relinquished all conquered territory.
