Bruma


Bruma during 3rd Era in Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion

Bruma is a Nibenese city located in the Jerall Mountains of Cyrodiil, north of the Imperial City. It is always relatively cold and snow-covered.

Architecture & Layout: The city's architecture is reflective of its primarily Nordic population. Bruma has a small sewer system which connects up with the natural caves that run beneath the town.

Quick History: In the game Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion, the Defense of Bruma became one of the most important conflicts of the Oblivion Crisis.
