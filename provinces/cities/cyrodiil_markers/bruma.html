Bruma

Bruma is a Nibenese city located in the Jerall Mountains of Cyrodiil, north of the Imperial City.

  • The city is always relatively cold and snow-covered.

  • The city's architecture is reflective of its primarily Nordic population.

  • Bruma has a small sewer system which connects up with the natural caves that run beneath the town.

  • The city is the seat of County Bruma.

  • In the game Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion, the Defense of Bruma became one of the most important conflicts of the Oblivion Crisis.
