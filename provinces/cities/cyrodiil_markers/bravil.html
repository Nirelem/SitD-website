Bravil

Bravil is a Nibenese city located on the banks of the Niben Bay, south of the Imperial City.

  • The Larsius River passes through and wraps around the central town.

  • Because of its location on the bay's shore and the marshes and swamps that surround it, the city's climate is damp.

  • By the Third Era, Bravil was a squalid town described as one of the poorest in Cyrodiil, with houses built like shacks, being wooden and stacked upon each other.

  • The Larsius River that runs through the town serves as Bravil's sewers.

  • The city is the seat of County Bravil.

  • During the Great War in 4E 171, invasion forces coming out of Elsweyr sieged and took the city after the fall of Leyawiin.

  • When the Great War ended, the Dominion relinquished all conquered territory.
