Bravil


Bravil during 3rd Era in Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion

Bravil is a Nibenese city located on the banks of the Niben Bay, south of the Imperial City.

Architecture & Layout: The Larsius River passes through and wraps around the central town. Because of its location on the bay's shore and the marshes and swamps that surround it, the city's climate is damp. By the Third Era, Bravil was a squalid town described as one of the poorest in Cyrodiil, with houses built like shacks, being wooden and stacked upon each other. The Larsius River that runs through the town serves as Bravil's sewers.

Quick History: During the Great War in 4E 171, invasion forces coming out of Elsweyr sieged and took the city after the fall of Leyawiin. When the Great War ended with the signing of the White-Gold Concordat, the Dominion relinquished all conquered territory.
