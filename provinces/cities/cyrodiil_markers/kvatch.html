Kvatch


Kvatch during 2nd Era in Elder Scrolls Online

Kvatch is a Colovian city in the hinterland of the Gold Coast of Cyrodiil, located between Anvil and Skingrad.

Architecture & Layout: It is built atop a large bluff, raising the city above the surrounding forests. The city is home to an arena, the imposing Kvatch Castle, and the Great Chapel of Akatosh.

Quick History: In the game Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion, Kvatch was the first city in Cyrodiil to be attacked at the outbreak of the Oblivion Crisis. The city would be rebuilt and restored to prominence just in time for it to be attacked again during the Great War in 4E 171.
