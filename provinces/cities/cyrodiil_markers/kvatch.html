Kvatch

Kvatch is a Colovian city in the hinterland of the Gold Coast of Cyrodiil, located between Anvil and Skingrad.

  • The city is built atop a large bluff, raising the city above the surrounding forests.

  • The city is home to an arena, the imposing Kvatch Castle, and the Great Chapel of Akatosh.

  • The city is the seat of County Kvatch.

  • In the game Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion, Kvatch was the first city in Cyrodiil to be attacked at the outbreak of the Oblivion Crisis.

  • The city would be rebuilt and restored to prominence just in time for it to be attacked again during the Great War in 4E 171.
