Anvil

Anvil is a prosperous Colovian harbor city on the Gold Coast by the Abecean Sea at the very southwest end of Cyrodiil. It is located at the end of the Gold Road.

  • The Redguards have had a notable influence on local architecture, which is of a similar style to that of nearby Hammerfell.

  • Anvil consists of five districts: Castle Anvil, Chapelgate, Westgate, Guildgate and Harborside.

  • In the hills outside of Anvil are streams with white poplar trees growing around them.

  • The city is the seat of County Anvil.

  • A large statue of a mermaid can be found in Guildgate district.

  • No one knows where the mermaid statue came from, and it remains a blank canvas upon which locals and visitors alike have painted their own picture.

  • Locals consider County Anvil to be premium hunting ground.

  • During the Great War in 4E 171, the city was put under siege by the armies of the Aldmeri Dominion, which ended with a Dominion victory in 4E 172.

  • When the Great War ended, the Dominion relinquished all conquered territory.

  • Circa 4E 180, Anvil Keep was a ruined structure inhabited by monsters.
