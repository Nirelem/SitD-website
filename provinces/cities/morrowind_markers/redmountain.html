Red Mountain

Red Mountain is a massive volcano that dominates the island of Vvardenfell in Morrowind. It's most recent eruption was in 4E 5, around 200 years prior to the present day, a catastrophe known as The Red Year due to the aftermath of the eruption being so devastating that its effects lasted for a year.

The Red Year eruption in particular was so devastating that Red Mountain's height was permanently reduced, along with the destruction of most of Vvardenfell, and other parts of Morrowind experiencing ash fall. The eruption caused widespread death along with a mass exodus of Morrowind's inhabitants.

Quick History: The following year on 4E 6, the disarray caused by Red Mountain's eruption caused the Argonians of Black Marsh to invade Morrowind, seeing it as the perfect opportunity to take revenge on the Dunmer for millennia of slavery.
