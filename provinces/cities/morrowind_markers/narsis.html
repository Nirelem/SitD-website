Narsis


Narsis during 2nd Era in Elder Scrolls Online

Narsis is one of the eight major cities on the mainland of Morrowind. The city is historically known as the ancient capital of the former great house of Hlaalu, which has been replaced with the Great House Sadras. From this city, they reign over west-central Morrowind.

Architecture & Layout: The city is located in a river basin centered around Lake Hlaalu, where it can be found on its south shore. It is built on a hillock that overlooks the lakefront to the north, and at the top of it lies the local kinhouse. However, the hill has a stone foundation built around it, providing ample space around it and elevation above the rest of the settlement. The city is surrounded by low-barring walls and much of the landscape is uneven ground; as a countermeasure, these hills are surrounded by stone platforms like under the kinhouse.

Quick History: Early in the Fourth Era, House Hlaalu was stripped of its position as a Great House and removed from the Grand Council of Morrowind, with its position and holdings transferred to the new House Sadras.
