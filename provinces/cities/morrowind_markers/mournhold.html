Mournhold


Mournhold during 2nd Era in Elder Scrolls Online

Mournhold, previously named Almalexia City, was the former capital city of Morrowind and the largest and oldest city in the province. Mournhold is located in the heart of the Deshaan region of Morrowind's southern mainland, and is the capital of House Indoril.

Politics: It was a temple-city, where the former Living-God Almalexia once resided, back when they were still in power.

Quick History: Almalexia, along with the other Living Gods, disappeared sometime after the events of Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind. In 4E 6, after the eruption of Red Mountain in 4E 5, Argonians invaded Morrowind and eventually sacked the city of Mournhold. The province's capital would then be moved to the city of Blacklight. By the time of the present day, the last traces of Almalexia have been removed from the temple, and the city is solely regarded as any other city in Morrowind.
