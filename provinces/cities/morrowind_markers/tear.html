Tear


Tear during 3rd Era in Elder Scrolls I: Arena

Tear is one of the eight major cities on mainland Morrowind. It is the capital city of the Great House Dres.

Architecture & Layout: The Tear region is notably hot and humid, given its proximity to Black Marsh and the greater Dres District, which mainly sweeps across the province's southern borderlands. Tear is the epicenter of a series of plantations, which are walled-off and heavily guarded by Dres spearmen.

Politics: Tear is the capital of the Great House Dres, whose sphere of influence revolves around an agrarian agricultural society, building its vast fortune from the export of saltrice and its grip on the slavery industry. Tear is also known as the Jewel of the East and it is the most open to trade and commerce compared to other cities on the mainland.

Quick History: When Red Mountain erupted in 4E 5, the ground around Tear turned into mush and the swamp essentially swallowed up half the city. Earthquakes tore through the ground and caused water to spill in until people were knee-deep in muck water. One year later, in 4E 6, the Argonians would launch their invasion of Morrowind. The Dunmer have been enslaving Argonians for millennia, but House Dres in particular cemented themselves as the linchpin of the Argonian slave trade. One can only imagine what must have happened in the capital city of Dres, so close to the border of Black Marsh, when the Argonians invaded.
