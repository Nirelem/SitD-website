Port Telvannis


Concept art of Port Telvannis

Port Telvannis is a city located on an archipelago in the Padomaic Ocean, northeast of Morrowind. It is described as an island palace under the control of Great House Telvanni, and is their capital city.

Architecture & Layout: The city is built in the Telvanni style of tall and majestic mushroom towers. These fungi take a thousand years to grow and have a special ecology of their own.

Politics: The Parliament of Bugs, the High Telvanni wizards who preside over the entire house, are headquartered here.
