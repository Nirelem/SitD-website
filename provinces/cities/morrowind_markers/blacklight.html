Blacklight


Blacklight during 3rd Era in Elder Scrolls I: Arena

Blacklight is one of the eight major cities on the mainland of Morrowind, situated on the northwest point of the province, near the Velothi Mountains.

Architecture & Layout: The town has hedge mazes in its parks and the city harbors are marked by wharves and piers. The city's region and its neighboring borderlands has been riddled with bandits and thieves since the early First Era, and are usually held up in caves throughout the mountains.

Blacklight is most well known as the capital of the Great House Redoran, whose values revolve around the virtues of maintaining honor and persevering through hard-work as a valiant warrior.

Quick History: Since the provincial capital, Mournhold, was sacked by the Argonians, in their invasion of Morrowind in 4E 6 after the eruption of Red Mountain in 4E 5, Blacklight has become the new capital and the Council of Great Houses have been held in the city. The city of House Redoran was chosen because it was the Redoran army that held back and drove off the invading Argonians.
