Archon

Archon is one of the eight major cities in the province of Black Marsh, situated on the eastern coast on the Padomaic Ocean.

  • The city is surrounded by dense marshland to the north and beaches to the south, near a river estuary.

  • Resort taverns in the city provide rooms with balconies where visitors can sit in the shade, drink, and admire the view of the city and surrounding area.

  • Archon is a major stop on the East Empire Company's southeast lines

  • The city has connections to the following cities: Lilmoth, Soulrest, and Tear.
