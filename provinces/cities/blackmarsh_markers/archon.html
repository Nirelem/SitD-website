Archon


Archon during 3rd Era in Elder Scrolls I: Arena

Archon is one of the eight major cities in the province of Black Marsh, situated on the eastern coast on the Padomaic Ocean.

Architecture & Layout: The city is surrounded by dense marshland to the north and beaches to the south, near a river estuary. Resort taverns in the city provide rooms with balconies where visitors can sit in the shade, drink, and admire the view of the city and surrounding area.

Politics: Archon is a major stop on the East Empire Company's southeast lines and with connections to the following cities: Lilmoth, Soulrest, and Tear.
