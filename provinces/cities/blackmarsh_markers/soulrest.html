Soulrest

Soulrest is one of the eight major cities in the province of Black Marsh, situated on the southwest coast next to the Topal Bay.

  • Soulrest is found on a peninsula that extends into the west, wrapping around Topal Bay.

  • There are also a number of islands found south of Soulrest and the mainland.

  • The city is a quiet port-town and its coastal access brings it a vast influx of foreign goods and aesthetics.

  • It was the capital of Black Marsh during the Third Era.
