Soulrest


Soulrest during 3rd Era in Elder Scrolls I: Arena

Soulrest is one of the eight major cities in the province of Black Marsh, situated on the southwest coast next to the Topal Bay.

Architecture & Layout: Soulrest is found on a peninsula that extends into the west wrapping around Topal Bay. There are also a number of islands found south of Soulrest and the mainland.

Politics: The city is a quiet port-town and its coastal access brings it a vast influx of foreign goods and aesthetics. It was the capital of Black Marsh during the Third Era.
