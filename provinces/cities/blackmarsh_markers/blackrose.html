Blackrose

Blackrose is one of the eight major cities in the province of Black Marsh, situated in a lake in the south-central portion of the province.

  • Blackrose is located on a large lake, where three rivers converge at once.

  • When the Imperial province of Black Marsh was established, the Imperials have commandeered the land and built over it.

  • Cities such as Blackrose are built in the Imperial style of architecture.

  • Blackrose is a city surrounded in infamy and contains many secrets.

  • It is said that many have entered its walls but few ever leave it.
