Blackrose


Blackrose Prison, outside Blackrose city, 2nd Era in Elder Scrolls Online

Blackrose is one of the eight major cities in the province of Black Marsh, situated in a lake in the south-central portion of the province. Blackrose is a city surrounded in infamy and contains many secrets. It is said that many have entered its walls but few ever leave it.

Architecture & Layout: Blackrose is located on a large lake, where three rivers converge at once. The Imperial province of Black Marsh was established, the Imperials have commandeered the land and built over it. Cities such as Blackrose are built in the Imperial style of architecture.
