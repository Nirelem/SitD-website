Gideon

Gideon is one of the eight major cities in the province of Black Marsh, situated in the flooded hillscape of Blackwood.

  • The city is surrounded by steep and lightly forested hills to the west and the humid marshland to the northeast.

  • The city is built majorly in the Imperial architectural style.

  • Most of the city's buildings are located on the main road because it's the only dry ground left through Gideon.

  • structures such as the Temple of Dibella (the Goddess of Beauty) and the Governor's Mansion can be found on the main road.

  • While the main road is large and spacious, the streets of Gideon are described as cramped and labyrinthine.

  • Gideon is a sleepy frontier town that serves as a gateway to the greater and treacherous expanse of the province.

  • The city is a mixture of two cultures, from the modern-day Imperials to the Argonians of Blackwood.

  • Gideon is often visited and settled by those that seek out Black Marsh's wonders.

  • Early in the 4th Era, Gideon was overrun by the undead forces of Umbriel, a floating island-city that required the souls of the dead to stay afloat. Fortunately, Gideon has mostly recovered as of the current day.
