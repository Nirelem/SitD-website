Stormhold

Stormhold is one of the eight major cities in the province of Black Marsh, serving as the largest settlement of the region of Shadowfen.

  • Stormhold is located near the banks of a mighty river that forms the provincial border between the primordial morass of Black Marsh and the scenic grazelands of Morrowind.

  • Even though the river is practically outside of the city walls, the land around Stormhold is sunken marshes and crocodile-infested ravines with shallow waters.

  • Much of the city is built on top of old ruins and wide-open mounds surrounded by ravines.

  • Stormhold is an Argonian settlement built on top of a former Dark Elf city, which was was built on top of Ayleid ruins.

  • The town uses both the stone, ornate architecture of the Dark Elves and the large mud huts of the Argonians.

  • When the Dark Elves were in control of Stormhold, they fully refurbished the site with proper stone roads, light posts, and long wooden bridges.

  • The local Hist Tree of Stormhold is located in the town square, in the middle of a pond, surrounded by ancient pillars, broken arches, and a small stone wall that circles around it.

  • Stormhold has a dark history that involves the nearby Dark Elves, as it was once the foothold of their slavery industry in the province.

  • Early in the 4th Era, Stormhold was overrun by the undead forces of Umbriel, a floating island-city that required the souls of the dead to stay afloat. Fortunately, Stormhold has mostly recovered as of the current day.
