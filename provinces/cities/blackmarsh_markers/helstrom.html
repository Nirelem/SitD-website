Helstrom

Helstrom is one of the eight major cities in the province of Black Marsh, located in the reputedly inhospitable center of the province called Middle Argonia.

  • The marsh of Helstrom is very dense and heavily consumed by Heart-Mud, which is reportedly pitch-black in color.

  • The city's alleys are considered both "wonderful and dangerous".

  • By the 3rd Era, Helstrom has become a fully incorporated city-state of the Third Empire, and a few settlements were even built in its interior.

  • Despite appearances, the city's placement in the center of Black Marsh, which is a region considered inhospitable to non-Argonians, has earned it the reputation of being the "immortal capital of the Argonians".

  • The city itself is notorious for its perilous reputation and the city's guard is reportedly dangerous.
