Thorn


Thorn during 3rd Era in Elder Scrolls I: Arena

Thorn is one of the eight major cities in the province of Black Marsh. Historically, the settlement has ties to the Dark Elf Great House of Dres, whose slave-raid on the city earned it its title as a Great House. Thorn earned the epithet of the Jewel of the East, and the town is just as deadly as it is beautiful.

Architecture & Layout: Thorn is located in the region of Thornmarsh. In the city's outskirts are fields of saltrice that House Dres have profited from since their occupation of the city.
