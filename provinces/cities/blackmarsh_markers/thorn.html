Thorn

Thorn is one of the eight major cities in the province of Black Marsh. Thorn earned the epithet of the Jewel of the East, and the town is just as deadly as it is beautiful.

  • Thorn is located in the region of Thornmarsh.

  • In the city's outskirts are fields of saltrice that House Dres profited from, back when they occupied the city.

  • Historically, the settlement has ties to the Dark Elf Great House of Dres, whose slave-raids on the city earned it its title as a Great House.
