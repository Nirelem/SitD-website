Elsweyr

Khajiit

Illustration of a Cathay Khajiit male

Summary:

  • Homeland of the Khajiit.

  • Makes up the south of Tamriel.

  • Divided into two parts: arid Anequina to the north and lush Pellitine to the south.

  • Both the kingdoms of Anequina and Pellitine are part of the Aldmeri Dominion.

  • Borders Cyrodiil to the north and east, and Valenwood to the west.

Real-world counterpart/inspiration:

South Asia/Southeast Asia (Indochina, India, Indonesia, and a bit of Gypsy/Romani)
