Oblivion Crisis


An Oblivion Gate

A Siege Crawler laying waste to a city

The Oblivion Crisis, which took place in Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion, was a cataclysmic event in the late Third Era (3E 433) when the Daedric Prince of Destruction, Mehrunes Dagon, attempted to invade Tamriel and bring about its destruction. Gates to Oblivion opened across the continent, unleashing Daedric (demons) armies that ravaged cities and landscapes. The crisis was ultimately resolved by Martin Septim, the last Septim Emperor, who sacrificed himself to become the avatar of Akatosh (Dragon God of Time) and banish Mehrunes Dagon, sealing the Oblivion gates permanently. This event marked the end of the Septim Dynasty and the Third Era.
