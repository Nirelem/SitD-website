Ayleids / Wild Elves


Ayleid ruins

Ayleid ruins (inside)

The Ayleids, also known as the Wild Elves, were the original Elven inhabitants of Cyrodiil and builders of its grand White-Gold Tower and other magnificent cities. Renowned for their mastery of light magic and architecture, they created a sophisticated civilization blending beauty and cruelty. The Ayleids worshipped Daedra (demons), often performing horrific rituals on enslaved humans, which ultimately led to their downfall. Their empire collapsed after the human rebellion led by Alessia, marking the rise of the First Empire of Men. Following their defeat, the Ayleids either assimilated into other Elven cultures or fled into obscurity, with remnants of their ruins and artifacts scattered throughout Cyrodiil.
