Snow Elves / Falmer


Falmer today

The last Snow Elf, amid Snow Elf ruins

The Falmer, or Snow Elves, were a once-proud and sophisticated race native to Skyrim, known for their mastery of magic and their ethereal beauty. Their civilization was devastated during the Nordic conquests, forcing them to seek refuge with the Dwemer , who ultimately betrayed them. The Dwemer enslaved the Falmer, feeding them toxic substances that blinded and corrupted them physically and mentally over generations. After the Dwemer's disappearance, the Falmer adapted to life underground, evolving into a feral and vengeful race, often attacking surface dwellers from their deep caves. Today, they are a shadow of their former selves, driven by hatred and survival, but remnants of their once-great culture linger in ancient ruins and legends.
