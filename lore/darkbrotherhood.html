Dark Brotherhood


The Black Hand, symbol of the Brotherhood

The Black Sacrament ritual

The Dark Brotherhood is a secretive and feared guild of assassins that once operated across Tamriel. They are bound by the Night Mother, a mysterious figure believed to guide the Brotherhood through her connection to the Void and the dread deity Sithis, the embodiment of chaos and death. Members of the Brotherhood follow a strict code, fulfilling assassination contracts granted through the ritualistic Black Sacrament performed by those seeking their services. Despite their secrecy, the organization has a long and infamous history, often embroiled in political intrigues and power struggles.

Over time, the Brotherhood has faced decline and internal conflict. One by one, their Sanctuaries throughout Tamriel were discovered and raided. By the present day, only one Sanctuary remains; the Dawnstar Sanctuary. The Dark Brotherhood is a shadow of its former glory. But with the last surviving Sanctuary, there is still hope that it may endure.
