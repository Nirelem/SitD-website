Dwarves / Dwemer


Concept art of Dwemer ruins

Dwemer Orrery from Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion

The Dwemer, also known as the Deep Elves or Dwarves, were an advanced and reclusive race of Mer who lived primarily in the mountainous regions of Morrowind , Skyrim, and Hammerfell . Renowned for their mastery of engineering, metallurgy, and arcane sciences, they constructed vast subterranean cities filled with mechanical automata powered by mysterious technology. The Dwemer were atheists compared to other races, rejecting traditional Aedra (gods) and Daedra (demons) worship in favor of logic and experimentation, even delving into dangerous realms of metaphysics. Their most infamous act was their attempt to harness the divine power of the Heart of Lorkhan , which is believed to have led to their sudden and unexplained disappearance during the First Era. Today, their ruins and artifacts, such as their automatons and Dwemer metal, remain a source of fascination and danger for explorers and scholars alike.
