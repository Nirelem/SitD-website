Warp in the West


The Iliac Bay before the Warp in the West

The four kingdoms of the Iliac Bay after the Warp in the West

The Warp in the West, also called the Miracle of Peace, occured during the conclusion of Elder Scrolls II: Daggerfall.

It was a mysterious phenomenon that took place between the 9th and 11th of Frostfall in 3E 417, and caused an "upheaval" in the areas surrounding the Illiac Bay region. For context, before the Warp, the Illiac Bay region was a complex network of dozens of city-states, minor kingdoms and fiefdoms, each with their own intricate web of alliances, petty squables, rivalries, and wars.

On the 9th of Frostfall, there were forty-four city-states in the Iliac Bay; on the 11th, after a number of mysterious cataclysms and anomalies, there were only four—Sentinel, Wayrest, Daggerfall, and Orsinium—all swearing loyalty to the Emperor.
