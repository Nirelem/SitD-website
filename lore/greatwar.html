The Great War


The Imperial City under siege

Dominion soldiers

The Great War was a devastating conflict fought between the Aldmeri Dominion , led by the Thalmor, and the Empire during the early Fourth Era (4E 171â€“4E 175). The war began when the Thalmor issued an ultimatum demanding the outlawing of Talos (God-Hero of Mankind) worship, the disbandment of the Blades (the elite unit of the Emperor's bodyguards), and other concessions, which the Empire refused. The Dominion launched a massive invasion, swiftly conquering Cyrodiil's southern regions and sacking the Imperial City. After years of brutal fighting, the Empire managed to reclaim the city but, weakened and unable to continue, signed the White-Gold Concordat, a peace treaty granting many of the Dominion's demands, including the suppression of Talos worship. The Great War's aftermath fueled resentment for the Empire in Skyrim, where the treaty's terms sparked the Stormcloak Rebellion.
